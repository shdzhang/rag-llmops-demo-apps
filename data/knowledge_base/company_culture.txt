# Company Culture and Values

## Our Mission

To empower organizations worldwide with innovative solutions that transform how they work, collaborate, and succeed.

## Core Values

### 1. Customer Obsession
We start with the customer and work backwards. Every decision considers customer impact first.

**In Practice:**
- Monthly customer advisory board meetings
- Customer NPS tracked and shared company-wide
- "Customer Voice" award for employees who go above and beyond
- Required annual customer visit for all employees

### 2. Innovation and Experimentation
We encourage bold ideas and learn from failures. Innovation isn't just for engineers—every role can innovate.

**Innovation Programs:**
- **Hack Weeks**: Quarterly week dedicated to passion projects
- **Innovation Fund**: $50K grants for employee-led initiatives
- **20% Time**: Spend one day per week on exploratory work
- **Failure Celebration**: Monthly "Learning from Failures" showcase

### 3. Inclusion and Belonging
We build a diverse team where everyone feels valued and heard.

**Diversity Metrics (2024):**
- 45% women in workforce (53% in leadership)
- 40% underrepresented minorities
- 15% LGBTQ+ employees
- Employees from 35+ countries

**Employee Resource Groups (ERGs):**
- Women in Tech
- Pride Alliance
- Black Excellence
- Asian & Pacific Islander Network
- Veterans and Allies
- Parents and Caregivers
- Neurodiversity Network

Each ERG receives $10K annual budget and executive sponsor.

### 4. Transparency and Trust
We default to open communication and assume positive intent.

**Transparency Practices:**
- All-hands meetings every Friday (CEO Q&A)
- Financial results shared quarterly with all employees
- Anonymous "Ask Me Anything" channel for leadership
- Open salary bands published internally
- Board meeting notes shared (where legally permissible)

### 5. Sustainable Excellence
We prioritize long-term success over short-term gains. Work-life balance is non-negotiable.

**Work-Life Balance:**
- No expectation to work evenings or weekends
- "No Meeting Fridays" for focus time
- Company-wide shutdowns: Last week of December and first week of July
- Mental health days encouraged (3 per year, no questions asked)
- "Unplug and Recharge" $1,000 annual vacation bonus (must disconnect fully)

## Communication Culture

### How We Work

**Async-First**
- Written documentation preferred over meetings
- 24-hour response time expectation (not immediate)
- Record meetings for global team members
- Use Loom videos for updates

**Meeting Culture**
- Default to 25 or 50-minute meetings (not 30/60)
- Agendas required for all meetings over 15 minutes
- "No agenda, no attenda" policy
- Meeting-free blocks: 9-10 AM and 2-3 PM daily

**Decision-Making**
- RAPID framework: Recommend, Agree, Perform, Input, Decide
- Documented in Confluence with stakeholder DRI (Directly Responsible Individual)
- "Disagree and commit" culture—voice concerns, then align

### Communication Tools

**When to Use What:**
- **Slack**: Quick questions, urgent matters, social chatter
- **Email**: External communication, formal requests, documentation
- **Confluence**: Long-form docs, project plans, decisions
- **Notion**: Team wikis, runbooks, onboarding docs
- **Zoom**: Face-to-face conversations, brainstorming
- **GitHub/JIRA**: Code reviews, bug tracking, sprint planning

## Career Development

### Growth Framework

**Career Ladders**
- Individual Contributor (IC) track: IC1 → IC2 → IC3 → IC4 → IC5 → IC6 (Principal/Fellow)
- Management track: M1 (Manager) → M2 (Senior Manager) → M3 (Director) → M4 (VP) → M5 (SVP/C-level)
- Switching tracks encouraged—no penalty for choosing IC over management

**Promotion Philosophy**
- Promote when already performing at next level (not potential)
- Promotion packets reviewed by calibration committee
- No forced curves—promote based on merit, not quotas
- Target: 20-30% of employees promoted annually

### Learning and Development

**Required Annual Training:**
- 40 hours of learning per year
- Managers: +20 hours of leadership training

**Learning Resources:**
- $1,500 personal learning budget
- Access to O'Reilly Learning, Pluralsight, Coursera
- Internal "Lunch and Learn" series (weekly)
- Mentorship program (optional)
- Executive coaching (Director level and above)

## Recognition and Rewards

### Peer Recognition
- **Kudos System**: Public praise in Slack with points (redeemable for gift cards)
- **Quarterly Awards**: $500 spot bonuses for exceptional work
- **Annual Awards**: Employee of the Year ($5,000 + trip)

### Compensation Philosophy
- Pay competitively at 75th percentile of market
- Annual merit increases (3-5% for meets expectations)
- Equity grants for all employees (refreshed annually)
- Transparent salary bands (published internally)
- Geographic pay adjustments based on location

### Bonus Structure
- Company performance bonus: 0-20% of salary
- Individual performance multiplier: 0.8x - 1.5x
- Paid quarterly to align with company results

## Work Environment

### Office Design
- Open floor plan with quiet rooms and phone booths
- Standing desks for all employees
- Ergonomic chairs and accessories
- Dual monitors standard (can request triple)
- Noise-canceling headphones provided

### Remote Work
- Hybrid-friendly: 2 days/week in office (core days: Tue/Thu)
- Fully remote roles available for certain positions
- Co-working space stipend: $200/month for remote employees
- Home office stipend: $500 one-time + $100/month for internet

### Sustainability
We're committed to carbon neutrality by 2030:
- LEED Platinum certified offices
- Renewable energy for all data centers
- Carbon offset for employee travel
- Electric vehicle charging stations (free)
- Bike-to-work incentive: $100/month

## Social Responsibility

### Corporate Giving
- **Matching Gifts**: Company matches donations up to $5,000/year
- **Volunteer Time Off**: 3 days/year for volunteering
- **Pro Bono Work**: 5% of engineering capacity for nonprofits
- **Foundation**: Company foundation donates 1% of profits to education and climate initiatives

### Supplier Diversity
- 30% of procurement spend with diverse suppliers
- Annual supplier diversity awards

## Health and Wellness

### Wellness Programs
- On-site gym and fitness classes (free)
- Wellness reimbursement: $500/year (gym, fitness classes, sports equipment)
- Standing desk converters available
- Healthy snacks and meals (subsidized)
- Annual flu shots and biometric screenings
- Mental health resources: Unlimited therapy via Modern Health

### Parenting Support
- Generous parental leave (16 weeks primary, 4 weeks secondary)
- On-site childcare center (subsidized 50%)
- $5,000 adoption assistance
- Lactation rooms in all offices
- Backup childcare service (20 days/year free)

## Employee Feedback

We measure culture through:
- **Quarterly Pulse Surveys**: 5-minute check-ins
- **Annual Engagement Survey**: Deep dive on culture, leadership, growth
- **Exit Interviews**: Learn from departing employees
- **Stay Interviews**: Proactively understand what keeps employees engaged

**Current Scores (2024):**
- Employee NPS: +65 (top quartile)
- Engagement Score: 82%
- Would Recommend: 91%
- Retention Rate: 93% (7% voluntary attrition)

## Living Our Values

### How We Hire
- Values-based interviews (30% of interview criteria)
- Diverse interview panels required
- Structured interviews with rubrics
- "No brilliant jerks" policy—values are non-negotiable

### How We Handle Conflicts
- Address issues directly with involved parties first
- Escalate to manager if unresolved
- HR mediation available
- Anonymous ethics hotline for serious concerns

### How We Celebrate
- Monthly birthday celebrations
- Quarterly team offsites
- Annual company retreat (all-expenses-paid)
- Milestone celebrations (anniversaries, launches, wins)
