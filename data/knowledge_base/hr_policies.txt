# Human Resources Policies and Benefits

## Parental Leave Policy

We offer comprehensive parental leave for all employees:

- **Primary Caregiver**: 16 weeks of fully paid leave
- **Secondary Caregiver**: 4 weeks of fully paid leave
- **Adoption**: Same benefits apply to adoptive parents
- **Flexibility**: Can be taken in multiple blocks within 12 months of birth/adoption

To request parental leave, submit a request through the HR portal at least 30 days before the intended start date.

## Remote Work Policy

Effective January 2024, all employees are eligible for hybrid work:

- **Minimum In-Office Days**: 2 days per week (Tuesday and Thursday are core days)
- **Full Remote**: Available for roles approved by department head
- **Equipment**: Company provides laptop, monitor, and $500 home office stipend
- **Expectations**: Maintain regular working hours (9 AM - 5 PM local time) and respond to messages within 2 hours

To request remote work arrangement:
1. Discuss with your manager
2. Submit formal request via WorkdayHR
3. IT will provision equipment within 5 business days

## Performance Review Process

Performance reviews occur bi-annually:

- **Mid-Year Check-in**: June (informal, no ratings)
- **Annual Review**: December (formal ratings and compensation adjustments)
- **Rating Scale**: Exceeds Expectations (5), Meets Expectations (3-4), Needs Improvement (1-2)
- **Self-Assessment**: Required 2 weeks before review meeting
- **360 Feedback**: Encouraged for all employees at Manager level and above

Promotion eligibility requires minimum 18 months in current role and "Exceeds Expectations" rating.

## Health Benefits

All full-time employees receive:

- **Medical Insurance**: Three plan options (Gold, Silver, Bronze)
  - Gold Plan: $0 employee contribution, $500 deductible
  - Silver Plan: $50/month, $1,000 deductible
  - Bronze Plan: $0 employee contribution, $2,500 deductible (HSA eligible)

- **Dental Insurance**: Fully covered by company
- **Vision Insurance**: Fully covered by company
- **Mental Health**: Unlimited therapy sessions through Modern Health

Open enrollment period: November 1-15 annually. New hires can enroll within 30 days of start date.

## Paid Time Off (PTO)

PTO accrual is based on tenure:

- **0-2 years**: 15 days per year
- **3-5 years**: 20 days per year
- **6+ years**: 25 days per year
- **Rollover**: Maximum 5 days can roll over to next year
- **Cash Out**: Not permitted except upon termination

Additionally, all employees receive:
- 10 company holidays
- 3 floating holidays (your choice)
- Unlimited sick days (manager approval for 3+ consecutive days)

## Professional Development

We invest in employee growth:

- **Learning Budget**: $1,500 per year for courses, certifications, conferences
- **Conference Attendance**: Up to 2 conferences per year (additional to learning budget)
- **Tuition Reimbursement**: 50% up to $5,000/year for degree programs
- **Internal Training**: Free access to LinkedIn Learning, Coursera, and Udemy Business

Submit requests through the Learning Management System (LMS) for approval.

## Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

Free, confidential support for:
- Mental health counseling
- Financial planning
- Legal advice
- Childcare and eldercare resources

Contact EAP hotline: 1-800-XXX-XXXX (available 24/7)

## Referral Bonus Program

Refer qualified candidates and earn:
- **Standard Roles**: $2,000 upon hire (paid after 90 days)
- **Hard-to-Fill Roles**: $5,000 upon hire (paid after 90 days)
- **Executive Roles**: $10,000 upon hire (paid after 90 days)

Submit referrals through the Careers portal. Bonus is taxable income.
