# New Employee Onboarding Guide

Welcome to our company! This guide will help you navigate your first 90 days.

## Pre-Start Checklist (Before Day 1)

You should receive these emails before your start date:

1. **IT Setup Email** (1 week before)
   - Instructions to set up laptop and accounts
   - VPN credentials
   - Badge pickup location

2. **Welcome Email from HR** (3 days before)
   - First day agenda
   - Parking information
   - What to bring (ID documents for I-9 verification)

3. **Manager Introduction** (1 day before)
   - Meet your onboarding buddy
   - First week schedule

## Day 1 Schedule

**8:30 AM - Badge Pickup**
- Security desk in main lobby
- Bring government-issued photo ID

**9:00 AM - Welcome Session**
- Meet HR team
- Complete paperwork (I-9, W-4, benefits enrollment)
- Receive welcome kit

**10:30 AM - IT Onboarding**
- Laptop setup and configuration
- Email and Slack account activation
- VPN and SSO setup
- Software installations

**12:00 PM - Team Lunch**
- Hosted by your manager
- Meet your immediate team

**2:00 PM - Workspace Tour**
- Office layout and amenities
- Conference room booking
- Kitchen and break areas

**3:00 PM - First Team Meeting**
- Introduction to team members
- Overview of current projects
- Q&A session

**4:30 PM - End of Day Check-in**
- Brief meeting with manager
- Questions and next steps

## First Week Priorities

### Required Training (Complete by End of Week 1)

All training is available in the Learning Management System (LMS):

1. **Information Security** (1 hour)
   - Data classification and handling
   - Password policies
   - Phishing awareness

2. **Code of Conduct** (30 minutes)
   - Company values
   - Ethics and compliance
   - Reporting concerns

3. **Workplace Safety** (30 minutes)
   - Emergency procedures
   - Health and safety policies

4. **Diversity and Inclusion** (45 minutes)
   - Inclusive workplace practices
   - Anti-harassment policies

### System Access Requests

Submit requests through IT Service Portal for:
- GitHub/GitLab repository access
- AWS/Azure/GCP accounts (if applicable)
- Salesforce, JIRA, Confluence
- Department-specific tools

Processing time: 1-2 business days

### Meet Your Onboarding Buddy

Your buddy is your go-to person for:
- Answering "simple" questions
- Navigating office culture
- Introducing you to key people
- Coffee chats and informal advice

Schedule weekly check-ins for your first month.

## First Month Goals

### Week 2-3: Learning Phase
- Shadow team members in daily work
- Review past projects and documentation
- Attend team standups and meetings
- Start contributing to low-priority tasks

### Week 4: First Assignment
- Receive your first project assignment
- Set 30-60-90 day goals with manager
- Schedule 1:1s with stakeholders
- Join relevant Slack channels and email lists

## First 90 Days Milestones

### 30 Days
- Complete all required training
- Understand team structure and key projects
- Deliver first small contribution
- First check-in with HR

### 60 Days
- Own a small project end-to-end
- Build relationships across teams
- Propose improvements or ideas
- Mid-point check-in with manager

### 90 Days
- Fully ramped on core responsibilities
- Delivering at expected level
- Formal review with manager
- Transition from "onboarding" to "fully integrated"

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: When do I get paid?**
A: Bi-weekly on Fridays. Your first paycheck will be 2-3 weeks after start date.

**Q: How do I request time off?**
A: Use WorkdayHR to submit PTO requests. Manager approval required.

**Q: What if I need to work from home?**
A: Notify your manager via Slack. No formal request needed for occasional remote work.

**Q: How do I submit expenses?**
A: Use Expensify app. Reimbursement within 2 weeks of submission.

**Q: Who do I contact for benefits questions?**
A: Email benefits@company.com or Slack #ask-hr

**Q: What's the dress code?**
A: Business casual. Jeans are OK. Dress up for client meetings.
